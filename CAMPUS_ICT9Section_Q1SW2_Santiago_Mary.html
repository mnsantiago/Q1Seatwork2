CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra) He is the only son of Don Rafael Ibarra, a wealthy and sophisticated Spaniard. He was born and raised in his home country, Philippines but had to spend 7 years studying in Europe which caused his obliviousness to the death of his father.
Maria Clara de Los Santos y Alba She was a representation of our country in Noli Me Tangere, she conjured up the images of an ideal Filipino woman and is the lover of Ibarra, she grew into a charming and beautiful woman raised by Catholic Sisters.
Don Santiago (Kapitan Tiago) He is the father of Maria Clara. He is very well known and has a lot of riches. He believes that worshipping saints will automatically give him a pass straight to heaven. He is the friend of Ibarra's father
