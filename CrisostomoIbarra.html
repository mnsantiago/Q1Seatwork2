Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra)


  • He is the only son of Don Rafael Ibarra, a wealthy and sophisticated Spaniard. He was born and raised in his home country, Philippines but had to spend 7 years studying in Europe which caused his obliviousness to the death of his father.
  • a very determined and earnest young man who is the fiancee of Maria Clara
  • loves his country very much and aims to improve it, to create a change for the better.
